Pirates Ahoy!

×

Pirates Ahoy!

    Rules & Regulations :
  • Maximum of three members per team.
  • Prelims may be conducted*.
  • The organizers will provide a map of our campus to each participating team, which indicates all the venues of the campus.
  • Each registered team will get a unique team name.
  • The Treasure Hunt consists of clues, each one leading to the next clue. Each correctly solved clue carries points. Totally there are 5 levels.
  • The clues are to be found in order. A team cannot skip a clue. Skipping a clue leads to disqualification of the team.
  • If you find other team’s clue by any chance never tamper it. A violation of this rule could cause disqualification of your team.
  • If you move any object to search the clue, kindly replace it back.
  • All team members of the respective teams must be present when they submit their final treasure.
  • The time taken to complete the task will be noted .
  • All the clues of the teams are different and located at different places but the final destination i.e. the treasure is same.
  • The team which finds the treasure wins first.
